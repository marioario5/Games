Dayang-Kushan Grand Bridge
By: GeordiFace



The Dayang-Kushan Grand Bridge is the longest bridge in the world, being 102 miles long. It is located in China, and is made up of connected bridges. One of them, the Jiaozhou Bay Bridge, crosses a bay conected to the ocean. It cost 8.5 BILLION dollars to make it.
